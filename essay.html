SPONSOR MY EDUCATION START-UP CONTEST

ABOUT CONTEST

This contest is about discovering and funding new innovative and social intervention start-ups. The program seeks to improve the social sector, creating more collaborative, people-driven and efficient processes. It is focused on students who are aspiring entreperneurs.

WHAT YOU CAN WIN

1ST PRIZE - ₵50,000

2ND PRIZE - ₵30,000

3RD PRIZE - ₵10,000

HOW TO PARTICIPATE

To participate, write and submit an essay with a minimum of 1500 words on a project or business you want to execute, detailing its qualities, impact and benefits to society. Your work should be convincing enough to be adjudge the best

HOW TO APPLY

Send a completed project with your name, email address, phone number, town/city, name of college/university to startups@sponsormyeducation.org

JUDGING CRITERIA

Your project for this sponsorship will be reviewed and judged based on your writing skills, style of execution of the project presentation and innovative business solutions described in your work, your mission and vision.
